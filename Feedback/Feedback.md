## Introduction

*Facilitator's opening script. Complete before the first question.*

- Thank the customer
- Confirm the time box (1 hour)
- Ask consent to record (internal reference only)
- Frame the purpose: Honest feedback, not sales or support
- Make sure the customer understands the specific product area being discussed
- Give everyone a chance to introduce themselves

## Questions

*Questions progress from broad to narrow per NN/g's funnel technique. Probes are listed for reference—skip any the participant has already covered.*

1. Tell me a bit about your role and what a typical week looks like for you.
2. Walk me through the last time you used [product name].
	- What were you trying to get done?
3. When [product name] can't help you, where do you go?
	- What does that source give you that [product name] doesn't?
	- How often does that happen?
4. Tell me about the most recent time [product name] let you down.
	- What did you do instead?
5. Are there situations where you'd expect [product name] to help, but it doesn't today?
6. If you could change or add one thing about [product name], what would it be?
	- Why is that important to you?
7. If [product name] went away tomorrow, what would you miss most?
8. Is there anything else you'd like to share about [product name]?
